# Nexus

### Multi-Agent Research Intelligence

> **4 specialized AI agents** collaborate in a verified pipeline to transform
> your research questions into citation-backed, fact-checked insights.

---

#### How it works

| Step | Agent | What it does |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | **Planner** | Decomposes your question into 3–5 focused sub-queries |
| 2 | **Retriever** | Searches arXiv + Semantic Scholar in parallel |
| 3 | **Synthesizer** | Builds a structured summary with citations |
| 4 | **Critic** | Fact-checks every claim — loops back if issues found |

#### Zero hallucination by design

Every claim in the final output is verified against source papers.
If the Critic finds unsupported claims, the Synthesizer revises — up to 2 rounds.

#### Powered by

LangGraph · Groq · arXiv · Semantic Scholar · Cross-Encoder Reranking · FAISS
